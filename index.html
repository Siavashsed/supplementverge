How We Grade

Our review methodology

Every rating follows the same process. No exceptions for popular ingredients, no exceptions for sponsors (we have none).

Step 01
PubMed Search
We start with a systematic PubMed search filtering for human RCTs. Animal studies and in-vitro research do not count toward the grade.
Step 02
Quality Assessment
We evaluate sample size, blinding method, placebo controls, funding source, and whether outcomes are clinically meaningful.
Step 03
Effect Size Analysis
Statistically significant does not mean meaningful. We report effect sizes and whether they matter to athletes and health users.
Step 04
Grade Assignment
A final letter grade reflects the overall body of evidence. Grades are updated when new trials publish - not left to decay.
